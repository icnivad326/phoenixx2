Cosmic Light Brush Effects

Figure 1. In this screenshot, selected the bride image layer and then clicked on the thumbnail while holding down the CTRL key. This action automatically selected the edges of the image; a new layer was created and named “ambient lights”.
  • According to the tutorial … the above procedure is necessary to paint lights on the body only.

  • Next, selected the eyedropper tool to sample the green and purple “flame” colors located at the foot of the subject. The selection had been modified and filled with the green_purple gradient to represent the reflection of light onto the subject's dress.
  • The final step was to set the ambient lights layer blending mode to “Color Dodge” with an opacity setting of 58% and a fill of 100%.
