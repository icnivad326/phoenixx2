Cosmic Light Brush Effects

In this screenshot, selected the bride image layer and then clicked on the thumbnail while holding down the CTRL key. This action selected the edges of the image, and then created a new layer named “ambient lights”.
  • According to the tutorial ... the above procedure is necessary to paint lights on the body only.

  • The final step was to set the ambient lights layer blending mode to “Color Dodge” with an opacity setting of 58% and a fill of 100%.

Colors in Motion & Cosmic Lights Effects1

This concept came about when updating a custom logo design for a NY Music Promotions Agency “Nicolandime Muzik”. This project started out with a tutorial to be used as a reference, “Show me the light” from psdbox.com. There were a few issues with the tutorial, and ended up using "trial and error" PS layers to achieve the results in the final composition.
