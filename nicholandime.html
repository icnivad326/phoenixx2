Nicholandime Jazz Bride

Colors in Motion & Cosmic Lights Effects1

This concept came about when updating a custom logo design for a NY Music Promotions Agency “Nicolandime Muzik”. This project started out with a tutorial to be used as a reference, “Show me the light” from psdbox.com. There were a few issues with the tutorial, and ended up using "trial and error" PS layers to achieve the final composition results.
