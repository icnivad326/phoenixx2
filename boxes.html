This project involved creating 4 different product sized boxes for a medical marijuana dispensary business. The original box design has curved edge box top flaps, to be sealed with a custom shaped “crack and peel” label with printed company logo. To add a personal touch to the design concept, a decorative string would be used to secure the box from all sides and tied at the top.

The client decided against the original box design concept. After extensive research and a better understanding of the clients’ specifications, came up with a universal box design. The universal box design would incorporate a previous project purple image background (including the company logo) to be printed on an HP Indigo press.

The slideshow (above) shows the four “big seller” container items to launch the box product line. The four items include: 2 different sized dram containers, a glass jar with metal lid, and a syrinch tube (approx. the height of a Cross Pen). The “cool” factor for me on this project involved “a hands-on” use of the engraving machine equipment to create the box parameter cuts and scores from my digital files.
